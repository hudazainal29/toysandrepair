Donation Campaign
This is one of the uniqueness in Toys and Repairs business. We are not only repairing and selling toys. But, we also donate to poor children and families.Toy and Repair businesses often carry out door-to-door donation activities to help people in need. For example, some customers come to the store and donate their child's toys that they no longer want to use. So, the toys will be repaired by us and we will donate them to poor children. The donations were given in terms of daily necessities, toys sponsored by Toy and Repair, as well as money of RM 800 for each family. Moreover, several customers come to our store location and offer clothes, stationery, and others to low-income families and children. Some also contributed in terms of money transferred to the Toys and Repairs' donation campaign We also provide an opportunity for anyone who would like to volunteer to participate in donation activities.
