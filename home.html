Background Information

Toys and Repairs

Toys and Repairs is a hybrid business established in 2017. Until now, the business is still standing intact and doing a variety of interesting products and services. Bandar Tun Huseinn Onn is our office location, and the business operates in The Mines Shopping Mall. Usually, people will throw away broken toys and choose to buy a new one, but those toys are still salvageable. So, we will offer services of repairing and giving new looks for the toys. And if the customers decided to donate their toys instead. We can resell them at a reasonable price for families with lower incomes. Thus, they also can provide toys for their children.

Vision, Mission and Objectives

Vision

To be a toys and repair company that can fulfill every dream of parents and children to own quality toys at affordable prices.

Mission

We will continue to improve and produce a unique and high-quality toy creation and reduce the disposal rate of toys that we can still repair.

Objectives

Hired a creative and innovative worker and reduce the burden of parents 'lives in providing the best for children
