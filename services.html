Service 2

Dinosaur (Repaint)

Everyone has their favourite toy that they will keep and try to preserve in various ways. However, in this case, this latex toy dinosaur has its bright colours faded away after being kept on the shelf for so long, therefore for this service, we will be giving new coats of colours, using poster paints mixed with white glue (to achieve the smooth texture and the shiny factor).


Here are the steps taken while enhancing the dinosaur latex toy:

  1. Dust of the dirt and specks of dust from the toy.

  2. Wash the toy with cold water
    (this is done to get rid of the mud and liquid-form stain)

  3. Let it dry, and apply (1 coat) of sanitizer and let it dry again.

  4. Apply top colour coat all over the dinosaur's body, similar to
    the dinosaur's skin tone and let dry

  5. Repaint the dinosaur's eyes with black paint (1 coat)

  6. Repaint the black stripes and spots on the dinosaur's body, apply 4 coats

  7. Repaint the dinosaur's edge sails with yellow mustard paint


    8. The price

    It depends on the size of the toy. Supposedly if the toy is bigger, then the price might increase. This is because the tool that we use is of high quality and expensive. However, we can consider if it is too costly for the customer

Service 4

Sew

Accidents happen, especially when there are children and toys involved. The joy of having your best friend (toy) playing together can sometimes end up with one getting hurt; most of the time, the toys are the victim. But do not fret; for this service, we will use our special sewing kits to enhance the joy in playing again; how stupendous!


Here are steps taken:


  1. Identify how many tears or broken buttons on the plush toy

  2. Examine whether it needs to be added more cotton inside of it

  3. Take the sewing kit and find the precise colour of thread to camouflage the stitches.

  4. Start sewing through the tears and lastly, lock the thread with a tie-off.


    5. The price

    We rarely receive toys or teddy bears that need to be sewed. Therefore, for one toy, we will only charge RM5.
